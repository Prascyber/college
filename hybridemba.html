HYBRID e MBA

Two-year + Two year, full-time — Affiliated to Bangalore University

A hybrid MBA(Two-Year) is a master of business administration program that combines online teaching methods with on-campus sessions.

The Hybrid MBA Program is a top-ranked mostly online work-compatible MBA option from University of Washington Foster School of Business. Taught by Foster’s high-caliber MBA faculty, this two-year, interactive program is designed for working professionals who need flexibility not offered by traditional MBA programs. The Hybrid MBA combines the rigor and reputation of a Foster MBA with the blended schedule of online coursework and quarterly in-person sessions.
